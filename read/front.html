Dear players Registration for round #7 is now underway.
Round start will be on Thursday 21:00h forum time.

Changes are:
  • No Furtifons or Samsonites.
  • Different FR rules.
  • Minor tweaks to certain Ops/Spells.
  • New UI and Planetary images.
  • Large galaxy.

    27 March 2013
    After enlisting the aid of my wife, neighbour and cat I have finally been able to discover how many of the 360 bad planets have been explored. No more than 70 planets with the bug are owned by you, the players.

    The crashes that are caused by people clicking these planets are annoying to say the least. I can also smell foul abuse from miles away, to hinder wars or just annoy players.

    The only solution I can come up with, is the following:

    Everyone with affected planets, will have these planets removed. In return, these players are granted 200 Tag Points.

    However, if you are feeling good hearted, you may also trade these 200 Tag Points and resources for a shiny Personalised Tag for this round AND the next, as a token of my appreciation.

    More details to follow.

    As always, thank you for playing.
